What About Us
Welcome to XBioscience

Established in 1989, Guybro Animal Health Pvt. Ltd. has swiftly become a leading integrated veterinary company in India. Our vision of a “Disease-Free Animal Future, Environmental Sustainability, and Global Health” drives our commitment to excellence.

We specialize in the manufacturing and marketing of high-quality feed supplements and disinfectants for Livestock, Poultry, Swine, and Aquaculture. Our products and services cater to clients across India and globally.

With a presence in over 11 countries, we are dedicated to expanding our global footprint while maintaining our commitment to advancing animal health, environmental sustainability, and global wellness.
